Summary:
1. Create a JetBrains learning account
2. Download WebStorm
3. During the set up remember to click .js .css .html .json 
4. Activate your WebStorm with your JetBrains Account
5. (Optional) Adjust the terminal with one that you desire, in my case bash.exe
6. Choose which interpreter you would like to use (example Node.js)
7. Use the specific interpreters keyword for running(example Node <program name>)
